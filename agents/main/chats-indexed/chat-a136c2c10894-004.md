### Local Deployment Options:
1. **Devstral Small 2 (24B parameters)**:
   - Runs on a **single RTX 4090** or a **Mac with 32GB+ RAM**.
   - Licensed under **Apache 2.0**, making it fully permissive for local use.
   - Ideal for solo developers or small teams with high-end GPUs.

2. **Devstral 2 (123B parameters)**:
   - Requires **4× H100-class GPUs** for full deployment.
   - Licensed under **Modified MIT**, which restricts commercial use for companies with **>$20M monthly revenue** without a commercial license.
   - Suitable for enterprises or teams with access to high-end hardware.

### Key Considerations:
- **Hardware Requirements**: Ensure your system meets the GPU and RAM requirements for the variant you choose.
- **Licensing**: Check the licensing terms, especially for commercial use.
- **Performance**: Local deployment may not match cloud-based performance, especially for the 123B variant.

### How to Deploy Locally:
1. **Download Weights**: Available on Hugging Face for both variants.
2. **Install Dependencies**: Use frameworks like `transformers` or `llama.cpp` for inference.
3. **Run Inference**: Use the Mistral Vibe CLI or integrate with your local development environment.

For most users, **Devstral Small 2** is the practical choice for local deployment due to its lower hardware requirements and permissive licensing.
