Within the Research Team container structure, the Researcher and Reporter agents form complementary roles. Researcher performs analytical research through Glob/Grep/Read/WebFetch tools and provides raw structured outputs. Reporter transforms these analytical outputs into polished, audience-appropriate documentation formats suitable for end-user presentation.
